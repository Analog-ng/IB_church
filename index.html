Welcome to

RGC Liberty Christian Centre

Praise the Lord Brethren, Greeting to you and your Family.

We wish you God’s blessings and grace as LCC family .

Our prayer for you is that the God of all grace anoints you with fresh oil and His precious Spirit illuminates your mind in understanding God’s perfect will for your life by His Spirit.

May the Lord empower you to effectively communicate to others the vibrant hope that He has given you as a believer.

May He cause you to persevere and cause you to overcome every struggle and challenge you may be encountering in Jesus name. Remember in all things you remain to be more than a conqueror. We welcome you to the family of LCC.

Feel free and share or recommend our page to your friends and network so that they can be blessed through this ministry.
